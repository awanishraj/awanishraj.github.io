About me

I was born in Patna(Bihar) and brought up all across India. But, I have spent the larger part of my life in Chennai and I absolutely love it.

I am pursuing a Dual Degree at Indian Institute of Technology Madras with B.Tech in Electrical Engineering and M.Tech in Microelectronics and VLSI.

College is the best thing that has ever happened to me. I gained new interests here, and lost a lot of weight! College introduced me to something that I can imagine doing my whole life - Tech! I have got my hands dirty with many things - mechanics, electronics, embedded design, aerobotics, android apps, web design, graphic design, video editing. And I am constantly looking for new things to do. I just love work and Sherlock describes me, "My mind rebels at stagnation. Give me problems, give me work!"
