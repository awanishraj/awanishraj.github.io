About me

I was born in Patna(Bihar) and brought up all across India. But, I have spent most of my life in Chennai and I absolutely love it.

I am pursuing a Dual Degree at Indian Institute of Technology Madras with B.Tech in Electrical Engineering and M.Tech in Microelectronics and VLSI.

College is the best thing that has ever happened to me. I gained new interests here, and lost a lot of weight! College introduced me to something that I can imagine doing my whole life - Tech! I have gotten my hands dirty with many things - mechanics, electronics, embedded design, aerobotics, android apps, web design, graphic design, video editing. And I am constantly looking for new things to do. I am a workaholic and in Sherlock's words, "My mind rebels at stagnation. Give me problems, give me work!"

I love watching movies, playing games(duh!). I also enjoy music and swimming. Food has been my passion always and I am an absolute non-vegetarian. Besides all this, my Lamborghini and my girlfriend have a lot in common. Hehe, no? It's Complex!
